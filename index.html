AureaBloom Sea Buckthorn Jaggery Powder

About the Product

AureaBloom Sea Buckthorn Jaggery Powder is a natural wellness supplement made from Sea Buckthorn berries blended with jaggery. It provides natural Vitamin C and antioxidant support for immunity and digestion.

Key Benefits

  • Supports natural immunity
  • Provides natural Vitamin C
  • Antioxidant support for vitality
  • Gentle on digestion

Who Should Use

  • People with low immunity
  • Individuals with fatigue
  • Those looking for natural Vitamin C support

Who Should Avoid / Use with Caution

  • Pregnant or breastfeeding women
  • People with autoimmune disorders
  • Diabetics (due to jaggery content)

Dosage

1 teaspoon once daily after meals with warm water or juice, or as advised by a healthcare professional.
