About the Product

AureaBloom Sea Buckthorn Jaggery Powder is a natural health supplement made from Sea Buckthorn berries blended with jaggery. It is a source of natural Vitamin C and antioxidants that support immunity, digestion, and overall wellness.

What is Sea Buckthorn Jaggery Powder?

Sea Buckthorn (Hippophae rhamnoides) is a nutrient-dense berry traditionally known for its antioxidant properties. AureaBloom combines Sea Buckthorn powder with jaggery to improve taste, digestive tolerance, and nutrient absorption.
